When I entered freelancing world

July 30, 2020 by Arvin

It was 2018 when I tried to join contest in Freelancer.com . It was a desperate move for me because during that time we are struggled financially. During that time, I don't have scholarships to help me in my studies. I won a contest in July 2018 which has a prize of 100 USD and after that I keep on trying to win other contest in that website until I got a bigger money. In 2020, I got a client from USA where he hired me as UI Designer for their website. This project is currently my biggest project and this is the first time that they are paying me per hour. To cut the long story short, freelancing help me to get experience since many companies today requires experience in hiring.
